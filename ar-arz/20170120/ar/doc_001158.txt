هولندا (توضيح)

هولندا Holland قد تشير إلى:


وتاريخياً قد تشير إلى:








A range of prototype and commissioned submarines were designed and built by، or named for، جون هولاند. The prototypes were designated using the Roman numerals (I–VI). The Holland VI prototype became the USS Holland (SS-1)، the first US Navy submarine. The British Royal Navy submarines based on designs by Holland were designated using the Arabic numerals (1–6).
